Good Intentions, Bad Choice

Obeying God all the way

Memory Verse

1 Samuel 15:22b-23a

Behold, to obey is better than sacrifice, and to hearken than the fat of rams. For rebellion is as the sin of witchcraft, and stubbornness is as iniquity and idolatry.

Main Applications

  • Obey God all the way, not just the easy parts.
  • Do not use good reasons to excuse bad choices.
  • Tell the truth instead of blaming others.
  • Obey God even when people want something else.
  • Remember that disobedience has consequences.
  • Good choices cannot save us. We need Jesus.
