Good Choices / Bad Choices

Daniel's Good Choice

Memory Verse - Daniel 1:8a

But Daniel purposed in his heart that he would not defile himself with the portion of the king’s meat, nor with the wine which he drank:

Find The Answers In Daniel 1

  1. What did the king command Ashpenaz to teach the young men?
  2. What new names were given to Daniel and his three friends?
  3. What did Daniel ask Melzar to give them instead of the king’s meat and wine?
  4. How many days did Daniel ask for the test to last?
  5. After the test, how did Daniel and his friends look?
  6. What did God give Daniel and his friends?
  7. How much better did the king find them than his wise men?
  8. How long did Daniel continue?

Main Applications

  • Decide to obey God before temptation comes.
  • The world often starts with small wrong choices.
  • Obey God even when it costs something.
  • Take a stand respectfully.
  • Trust God with the results.
  • Small choices reveal big loyalties.
  • Good choices do not save us. We need Jesus to save us from sin.
My purpose this week:
I will choose to obey God when ____________.
