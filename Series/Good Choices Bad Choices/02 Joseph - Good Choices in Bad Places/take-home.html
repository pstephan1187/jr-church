Good Choices in Bad Places

Choosing right when it is not fair

Lesson Truth

Even when life is unfair, God still sees, God still works, and we should still choose to do right.

Memory Verse

Genesis 50:20

But as for you, ye thought evil against me; but God meant it unto good, to bring to pass, as it is this day, to save much people alive.

Main Applications

  • Do right even when others do wrong to you.
  • Do right even when nobody seems to notice.
  • Do right even when you are blamed unfairly.
  • Do right when you are tempted.
  • Serve others even when you are hurting.
  • Forgive instead of taking revenge.
  • Good choices cannot save us. We need Jesus.
