# A Flooded Boat Image Plan

- **Series:** Adventures in the Bible
- **Lesson:** A Flooded Boat
- **Passage:** Matthew 8:23-27
- **Style source:** `../series-image-style.md` and `cover-graphic.png`

1. `image-1.png` - On a sunny shore, Jesus steps into the boat with His disciples close behind while distant clouds hint at a possible storm; the peaceful departure establishes that they are following Jesus even though they cannot see what is ahead.
2. `image-2.png` - A sudden great tempest overwhelms the fishing boat while experienced disciples strain at the ropes and bail water; the scene shows that human skill cannot control every danger.
3. `image-3.png` - Jesus sleeps peacefully in the stern while terrified disciples wake Him and plead for help amid the raging storm; the contrast teaches children to call on the Lord honestly when afraid.
4. `image-4.png` - Jesus stands securely in the storm and commands the wind and sea while the disciples watch; the water is visibly changing from chaos to a great calm under His authority.
5. `image-5.png` - In bright stillness after the storm, the disciples marvel at Jesus as the glassy sea and resting sail show the complete calm; the conclusion centers on who Jesus is and why He can be trusted.
