# From Tombs To Testimony Image Plan

- **Series:** Adventures in the Bible
- **Lesson:** From Tombs To Testimony
- **Passage:** Mark 5:1-20; Luke 8:26-39; Matthew 8:28-34
- **Style source:** `../series-image-style.md`, series `cover-graphic.png`, and prior Adventures lesson images
- **Image count:** 6 scene images, one for each lesson point.
- **Shared note:** Ordinary lesson images should have no embedded text, title, or series logo.

1. `image-1.png` - Point 1: The man among the tombs, disheveled and distressed but not graphic, with broken chains and frightened villagers keeping their distance as Jesus' boat reaches the shore.
2. `image-2.png` - Point 2: A deeper buildup scene showing the man isolated near the tombs with the warm city/home life far behind him, suggesting the wrong road pulling him farther from God without inventing a specific backstory.
3. `image-3.png` - Point 3: Jesus meets the man with compassion; the man falls before Him while dark shadowy forms recoil in fear, showing the devils begging before Christ's authority.
4. `image-4.png` - Point 4: Jesus casts out the devils; dark wisps leave the man toward the herd of swine rushing down the hillside toward the sea, with the man beginning to be freed.
5. `image-5.png` - Point 5: The changed man sits clothed and calm near Jesus, in his right mind, while townspeople stare in astonishment at the visible difference.
6. `image-6.png` - Point 6: The changed man joyfully tells friends and neighbors in Decapolis what Jesus did for him, with a bright hopeful village scene and clear testimony energy.
