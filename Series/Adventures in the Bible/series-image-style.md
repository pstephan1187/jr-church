# Adventures in the Bible Image Style

- **Logo file:** `series-logo.png`
- **Reusable image assets:** `image-assets/`
- **Logo format:** transparent PNG that fits comfortably inside a roughly square placement area
- **Logo silhouette:** stacked adventure-map emblem with open Bible base, arched title banner, compass sunburst crown, wave/ship framing, and sticker-style outer outline
- **Series cover file:** `cover-graphic.png`
- **Previous cover attempt:** `cover-graphic-attempt-1.png`
- **Series image canvas:** 16:9 PNG, preferred 1672 x 941
- **Art style:** polished kid-friendly adventure illustration with dimensional cartoon rendering, clean silhouettes, thick white outlines, and classroom-screen readability
- **Palette:** parchment tan, golden yellow, ocean blue, sky blue, warm red, leafy green accents, stone gray, and deep navy outlines
- **Lettering:** fully integrated illustrated wordmark treatment; `ADVENTURES` is large arched orange-gold dimensional lettering built into a navy banner, `in the` is hand-lettered on a parchment scroll with no red backdrop, and `BIBLE` uses large individual adventure-textured block letters with bright colors, bevels, white sticker outlines, and deep shadows
- **Motifs:** open Bible as adventure map, compass/sunburst, sling stone, ocean wave and wooden ship, trumpet, stone wall, shield, banner, foliage, and journey-path details
- **Cover composition:** text-only series wordmark centered over a bright sky-glow and journey-path scene, using the logo lettering style without repeating the full logo emblem; wave/ship and open-map Bible frame the left side, stone city/battle-hill and shield/trumpet frame the right side, with edge detail kept outside the main title zone
- **Lighting and mood:** bright, hopeful, brave, adventurous, and child-appropriate with warm highlights and no dark or scary atmosphere
- **Do reuse:** thick sticker outlines, parchment/map textures, blue-and-gold adventure palette, simple symbolic Bible-story props, and broad readable title/safe areas
- **Avoid:** grim battle scenes, gore, frightening storm imagery, photorealistic adults, cluttered tiny props, extra embedded text, misspelled lettering, and dark dramatic lighting
