# The Tent Peg Victory Image Plan

- **Series:** Adventures in the Bible
- **Lesson:** The Tent Peg Victory
- **Passage:** Judges 4-5
- **Style source:** `series-image-style.md`, the Ehud/Eglon lesson cover, and this lesson's `cover-graphic.png`
- **Shared note:** Ordinary lesson images should have no embedded text, title, or series logo.

1. `image-1.png` - Quick review/context: Israelite families and children hear the story of Ehud's victory during the long years of rest, setting up the repeated Judges cycle.
2. `image-2.png` - Point 1: Sisera's iron chariots loom over frightened Israelites as they cry to the LORD, showing that sin brought trouble again.
3. `image-3.png` - Point 2: Deborah sits under her palm tree and gives Barak God's command and promise, showing that God's Word can be trusted.
4. `image-4.png` - Point 3: Barak and Israel go down from Mount Tabor while Sisera's chariots struggle near the Kishon, showing that God is greater than the strongest enemy.
5. `image-5.png` - Point 4: Sisera runs exhausted to Jael's tent, and Jael gives him milk and a covering while he tries to hide from God's judgment.
6. `image-6.png` - Point 5: Jael courageously stands on the LORD's side, meeting Barak at the tent after the victory with the hammer and tent peg visible, while the scene stays non-graphic and child-appropriate.
