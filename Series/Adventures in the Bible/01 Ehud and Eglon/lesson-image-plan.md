# The Secret Strike Image Plan

- **Series:** Adventures in the Bible
- **Lesson:** The Secret Strike
- **Passage:** Judges 3:12-30
- **Style source:** `../series-image-style.md`, `../cover-graphic.png`, and `cover-graphic.png`
- **Image count:** 5 scene images, matching the five actual lesson-outline points.
- **Style override for this lesson set:** keep the polished adventure illustration style, but do not use white sticker-style outlines around characters or objects.
- **Character lock:** Ehud should match `cover-graphic.png` as a grown Israelite man in the blue-and-cream outfit, with adult proportions and a mature face; do not depict him as a child or young teenager. Eglon should be a very fat Moabite ruler in purple robes.

1. `image-1.png` - Israel forgets God and suffers under Moab: Israelite families near the city of palm trees with Moabite tribute collectors, showing consequences without making the scene frightening.
2. `image-2.png` - Israel cries unto the LORD: a hopeful prayer scene with weary Israelite families and elders turning back to God after years of oppression.
3. `image-3.png` - God raises up unexpected Ehud: left-handed Ehud preparing the tribute basket and concealed dagger before approaching Eglon's palace.
4. `image-4.png` - Ehud faces the enemy with courage: tense, non-graphic private chamber moment as Ehud gives his message to powerful King Eglon.
5. `image-5.png` - Ehud sounds the trumpet and God gives rest: Ehud on the mountain blowing the trumpet while Israelites gather with courage and bright hope.
